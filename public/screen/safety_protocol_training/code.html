On-site Protocol Sequence

1

Initial Scene Survey

Before entering any customer residence, perform a 360-degree exterior scan for visible gas vents, structural damage, or unsecured pets. Look for the "Safety Three": Sparks, Smells, and Sounds.

2

Client Verification & Communication

Establish clear communication with the homeowner. Identify yourself using the official One Call ID and explain the safety perimeter you will be establishing during the repair process.

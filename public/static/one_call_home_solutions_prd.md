# One Call Home Solutions - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
**Name:** One Call Home Solutions
**Tagline:** "Your Safety Home Our Priority."
**Industry:** Home Services & Facility Management
**Target Device:** Mobile

## Core Value Propositions
- 24/7 Emergency Support (30-60 min response)
- 300+ Services across 28+ Divisions
- Trained & Verified Professionals
- Seamless Digital Booking Experience

## Key Screen List

### 1. Home / Explore Screen
- Brand Header with Location Selector
- Search Bar (covering 300+ services)
- Emergency "One Call" SOS Button
- Service Categories Grid (Cleaning, Painting, Plumbing, etc.)
- Business Metrics (Trust signals: 300+ services, 24/7 support)
- Promotional Banners for AMC / Seasonal offers

### 2. Service Catalog (Category Level)
- Detailed breakdown of sub-services (e.g., Cleaning > Bathroom, Kitchen, Sofa)
- Price Estimator preview
- Key features (Safety, Quality Guarantee)

### 3. Booking & Scheduling Flow
- Multi-property selection (Home/Office)
- Date and Time Slot selection
- Payment Method integration (UPI, Card, EMI, COD)
- Service Warranty (7-30 days) highlights

### 4. Technician Tracking & Emergency SOS
- Live Map view
- Technician Profile (Verified badge, Photo, ID)
- OTP Verification UI
- Emergency Hotline shortcut

### 5. Account & Property Dashboard
- Multi-property management
- Service History & Digital Invoices
- AMC Subscription Management (Quarterly inspections, Priority Queue)
- Loyalty Points & Rewards

## Design Direction
- **Primary Color:** Trustworthy Blue or Safety Green/Orange
- **Secondary Colors:** Clean White/Grey for "Home" feel
- **Typography:** Professional Sans-Serif (Inter/Roboto)
- **Imagery:** Real professional workers, clean interiors
- **UX Patterns:** Low-friction booking, clear "Verified" status indicators
